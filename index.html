2TR Football Rulz

The 2TR Football game has been designed by 3-2-WON LTD, a team of experienced football coaches from the Highgate Newtown Community Centre in Camden North London.

Charles Browne and Michael Lyle worked to develop the game over a series of years. The first official 2TR Football tournament was held in North London, in August 2007 on the Whittington Estate Football Pitch and since then the game has grown in popularity with cross borough tournaments being very successful now 2TR Football has gone national

2TR Football Game Features

The 2TR Football game is simple enough for any player to grasp, but designed to provide the greatest amount of development in both the individual player and the team

  • Limit to 2 consecutive touches per player
  • 4 Players per side
  • 3-7 Substitutes
  • No traditional goalkeeper
  • No scoring from own half
  • Goals in area from volley only
  • 15 second restart after goal scored
  • 2TR Penalties and Free-kicks
  • Forces team play
  • New strategies
  • Fast paced
  • No ball hogging
